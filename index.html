    We are specialized in Parking lot Enforcement of your Private and Commercial Property in and all around Houston, Texas.We are family owned. We also Specialize in Heavy Duty Towing!

    Violations for which a vehicle can be towed immediately without warning include:
    - Parking in a fire zone, tow-away zone, or no parking zone!
    - Parking in a handicapped space without proper identification!
    - Parking in a manner that blocks another vehicle or double-parked!
    - Parking in a manner that blocks a dumpster!
    - Parking on the grass, sidewalk, or curb!
    - Parking in another resident's assigned space!
    - Parking in a space that requires a permit without a valid permit!
    - Any vehicle found in a condition which may cause damage or pose a threat to persons or the property (jack stands, leaking gas or other hazardous fluids, etc.)!
